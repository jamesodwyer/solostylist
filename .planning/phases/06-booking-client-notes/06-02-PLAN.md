Present the verification steps to the user and wait for their response.

What was built: Client notes and colour formula preview integrated into BookingSheet (services step) and AppointmentSheet (view mode). Notes are fetched via browser Supabase client when a client is selected or an appointment is opened. Display uses a shared ClientNotesPreview component with collapsible section, colour formula highlight, and "View all" link.

Verification steps for the user:

Prerequisites: Have at least one client with notes and/or a colour formula saved (via the client detail page Notes tab or Colour tab).

1. Open the app on mobile or in responsive mode (375px width).
2. Go to the Diary page.
3. Tap an empty time slot to open the BookingSheet.
4. Search for and select the client who has notes.
5. On the Services step, verify:
   a. A "Client Notes" collapsible section appears below the header.
   b. It defaults to collapsed (just the header bar visible).
   c. Tap to expand — colour formula (purple card) and recent notes (grey cards) are visible.
   d. Notes content is truncated at 3 lines (not overflowing).
   e. A "View all" link appears at the bottom of the expanded section.
   f. The service list is still easily scrollable below the notes section.
6. Go back to client selection, select a different client (or the same one again).
7. Verify notes reset correctly (no stale data from previous client).
8. Complete a booking for the client with notes.
9. Tap the newly created appointment in the diary to open the AppointmentSheet.
10. In view mode, verify:
    a. Below the "Notes" section (appointment notes), a "Client Notes" collapsible section appears.
    b. Expanding it shows the same colour formula and notes from the client's record.
    c. "View all" link navigates to the client detail page.
11. Verify the notes section does not push action buttons off screen on a small phone (375px width).

Resume signal: Type "approved" if all checks pass, or describe any issues found.

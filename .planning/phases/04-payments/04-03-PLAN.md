<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>Complete Phase 4 payment system: payment recording from appointment sheet, refund/void adjustments, Money page with daily totals and date navigation, and payments in client timeline.</what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
Test the full payment flow:

1. **Record a payment:**
   - Open the diary, find or create a completed appointment (tap an appointment, mark as "Complete")
   - In the appointment sheet, tap "Take Payment"
   - Verify: PaymentSheet opens with amount pre-filled from appointment services total
   - Select "Cash", confirm amount, tap "Record Payment"
   - Verify: Sheet closes, appointment sheet now shows "Payment recorded" instead of "Take Payment"

2. **Check Money page:**
   - Navigate to the Money tab (bottom nav)
   - Verify: Today's date is shown, the payment you just recorded appears in the transaction list
   - Verify: Cash total shows the payment amount, Net total matches
   - Tap date arrows to navigate to yesterday -- verify no payments (or previous payments if they exist)
   - Navigate back to today

3. **Record a card payment:**
   - Complete another appointment and take payment, this time selecting "Card"
   - Verify: Money page now shows both Cash and Card totals

4. **Record a refund:**
   - On the Money page, find a payment and tap "Refund"
   - Verify: AdjustmentSheet opens with original payment details
   - Select "Refund", adjust amount if desired (partial refund), add a note, confirm
   - Verify: Refund appears in the transaction list with red badge
   - Verify: Net total is reduced by the refund amount (Gross - Refunds = Net)

5. **Record a void:**
   - On the Money page, find a payment and tap "Refund", then select "Void (cancel)"
   - Verify: Amount is locked to full original amount (read-only for void)
   - Confirm the void
   - Verify: Void appears in the transaction list with amber badge

6. **Check client timeline:**
   - Navigate to Clients tab, open the client you recorded payments for
   - Tap the "Payments" tab
   - Verify: All payments (including refunds/voids) appear with correct amounts and timestamps

7. **Edge cases:**
   - Try to record a payment with 0 or empty amount -- should show validation error
   - Open a completed appointment that already has a payment -- should show "Payment recorded" not "Take Payment"
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all checks pass, or describe any issues found.</resume-signal>
